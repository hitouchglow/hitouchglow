Our Services

Expert body shop restoration and vehicle maintenance services tailored to manufacturer standards.

Major & Minor Denting

Advanced sheet metal pullers and structural dent removal to return body panels to factory shape.

Major & Minor Tinkering

Precision alignment, metal stitching, structural frame tinkering, and welding repairs.

Full Body Painting

State-of-the-art paint booth application with premium materials for a pristine, original clear-coat finish.

Panel Painting

Exact color matching and localized panel spraying for doors, hoods, fenders, and quarter panels.

Scratch & Dent Removal

Quick repair system for surface scratches, key marks, shopping cart dings, and localized paint touchups.

Bumper Repair & Painting

Plastic bumper welding, crack repairs, and specialized flexible painting to prevent future peeling.
